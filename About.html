SRG Imperial Grand hotel is a partnership business owned by two partners named Atkins Robert and George Wilberforce. It was built in the year 1998 and has been in existance for more than 25 years now. It is one of the largest and well known hotels in Entebbe due to its way of delivering services. It is estimated at USD 200 million as per 2025, with George Wilberforce as the main pivotal behind this achievement.
